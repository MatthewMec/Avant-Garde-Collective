Visual Arts, Drama & Creative Expression

Intro to Art

Grades 1-3

Through a diverse range of projects and techniques, early learners will explore foundational art concepts such as color, shape, texture, and composition, while also engaging in more complex creative challenges tailored to their experience level. Younger students will develop fine motor skills and creative confidence through fun, hands-on activities.

Color Theory Fine Motor Skills Creative Expression

Art for Kids

Grades 4-6

This inclusive art class welcomes students of all skill levels. Through a diverse range of projects and techniques, students will explore foundational art concepts such as color, shape, texture, and composition, while also engaging in more complex creative challenges tailored to their experience level. Intermediate students will expand their artistic vocabulary and experiment with various mediums.

Mixed Media Artistic Vocabulary Composition

Drawing

Grades 6-12

Explore the dramatic, expressive world of pencil and charcoal. Learn foundational drawing techniques like shading, blending, and creating tone to develop works of art in portraits, still lifes, perspective and more. This course is perfect for artists looking to improve their technique and skill.

Pencil & Charcoal Shading Techniques Portraiture Perspective Drawing

Drama

Grades 5-12
Minimum 20 participants required

With a focus on improv and development of an actual play, this class will have students work through the fundamentals of the dramatic experience culminating in the full production of a play. Students will develop performance skills, character development, stage presence, and collaborative teamwork while bringing stories to life.

Improvisation Character Development Stage Performance Script Analysis Teamwork
