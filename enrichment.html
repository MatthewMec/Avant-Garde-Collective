Pre Algebra

Grades 6-9

Do you want math to be more than numbers on a screen or page? This class will give the instruction needed to navigate high school algebra with ease using one-on-one help and practical real-life examples to make math fun! Students will master essential mathematical concepts through hands-on activities and personalized instruction.

Mathematical Reasoning Problem Solving Real-World Applications Individual Support

Business Leadership

Grades 9-12

A business class introduces young entrepreneurs to fundamental business concepts through interactive activities. Participants learn how to brainstorm and develop a product or service, create a basic business plan, understand costs and profit, and market their ideas. Students will actually engage in a real life business which will be affected by the choices they make. This class will be a prerequisite to participate in our student store.

Entrepreneurship Business Planning Financial Literacy Marketing Leadership
Real Business

Lego Club

Grades 2-12

Lego Club brings problem-solving, teamwork, fine motor skills and creativity together in a combination of collaboration and play. Collaborative group builds will be teams working together to complete a complex set or build a large-scale project with certain criteria to meet. Free-building sessions will dedicate time for unstructured play, allowing kids to create whatever they can imagine with a large bin of bricks.

Creative Problem Solving Teamwork Fine Motor Skills Spatial Reasoning Engineering Concepts
