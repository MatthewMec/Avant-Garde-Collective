Fun with Spanish

Grades K-5

This class offers a fun environment to learn a new language. We will play games while learning new words in Spanish with worksheets from Carsondellosa.com Starter Spanish book. Students will learn colors, shapes, numbers, animals, house structure and furnishings, foods and everyday greetings through interactive activities and play-based learning.

Basic Vocabulary Interactive Games Cultural Awareness Pronunciation

Spanish I

Grades 6-12

In this first year class we'll learn about the Spanish Language and culture. We will practice reading, play games, and listen to songs to help students feel comfortable learning a new language. Students will become familiar with Spanish words and, with some work, hopefully begin to understand and speak the Spanish language through immersive and engaging activities.

Reading Comprehension Basic Conversation Cultural Studies Grammar Fundamentals
Foundation

Spanish II

Grades 6-12
Prerequisite: Spanish I or equivalent

With a prerequisite of a previous Spanish class, we will continue learning and practicing the Spanish language, focusing more on conversational skills and using past, present and future tenses. Students will develop greater fluency through advanced grammar, extended conversations, and cultural exploration.

Advanced Grammar Verb Conjugation Fluent Conversation Cultural Immersion

Mock Trial

Grades 9-12

Develop public speaking and analytical skills in this mock trial course. Students will dive into the American judicial system by taking on roles as lawyers, witnesses, and judges in a simulated courtroom setting. The class covers trial procedures, rules of evidence, and key areas of criminal and constitutional law while building confidence in public presentation and logical argumentation.

Public Speaking Legal Analysis Courtroom Procedure Critical Thinking Debate Skills
Advanced

Book Writing

Grades 9-12

Unlock your imagination in this creative writing course for young authors. Learn the fundamentals of storytelling, including character development, plot structure, and setting, as you draft your own book idea. This class provides a supportive environment for developing your writing skills and finding your unique voice while completing a substantial creative work.

Creative Writing Character Development Plot Structure Storytelling Voice & Style
